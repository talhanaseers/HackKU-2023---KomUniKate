KomUniKate: Your Personal AI for Safe and Productive Online Conversations

KomUniKate is a web application that uses artificial intelligence and machine learning to filter out inappropriate comments in real-time. It analyzes users' comments and flags them based on six attributes, each of which is described below:

The Six Attributes Used by KomUniKate

  1. Toxicity: This attribute measures the degree to which a comment is likely to make others feel uncomfortable or offended. It includes hate speech, discriminatory language, and any statement that suggests prejudice or bias towards a particular group of people.
  2. Insult: This attribute measures the use of language intended to offend someone. It includes name-calling, abusive language, or any statement that is designed to ridicule or belittle a person.
  3. Profanity: This attribute measures the use of vulgar, obscene, or offensive language. It includes swear words, sexual content, or any statement that is deemed inappropriate for a public forum.
  4. Threat: This attribute measures any statement that suggests harm or danger to someone. It includes threats of violence, harm to a person, or any statement that is intended to cause fear or alarm.
  5. Attack: This attribute measures any statement that attacks a person or group of people. It includes personal attacks, insults, or any statement that is intended to cause harm to someone's reputation or well-being.
  6. Identity Attack: This attribute measures any statement that attacks a person based on their identity. It includes discriminatory language against someone based on their race, gender, or sexual orientation.

With KomUniKate, you can enjoy safe and productive online conversations without worrying about inappropriate comments. To get started, click here to type your comment.
